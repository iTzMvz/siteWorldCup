World Cup 2022
the top goalscorers

The Golden Boot will be awarded to the player who scores the most goals at Qatar 2022. If two players are equal, the one with the most assists will win it. If two or more players are still equal, the one who has played the fewest minutes will triumph.

Bookings and suspensions
Who is in danger of missing out?

A player who receives a second yellow card during the tournament is suspended for the following game. After the quarter-finals, any yellow cards collected up to that point will be annulled, meaning a player cannot miss the final if they are booked.
